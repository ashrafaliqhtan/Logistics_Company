Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles do you offer for transportation?

We offer a wide variety of vehicles including trucks, vans, and specialized vehicles to accommodate different logistical needs.

What areas do you serve?

We serve cities across the entire country, providing both local and long-distance transportation services for businesses.

How can I request a quote for my transportation needs?

You can easily request a quote by visiting our pricing page or contacting us directly through the contact form.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfers, and online payment gateways such as PayPal.

Do you offer tracking for my shipments?

Yes, we offer real-time tracking for all shipments. You will receive a tracking number once your shipment is dispatched.

Can I reschedule or cancel a transportation request?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel a request, but please do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any fees.

What should I do if my shipment is delayed?

If your shipment is delayed, please contact our customer service immediately for assistance. We will keep you updated on the status of your shipment.

Are your services insured?

Yes, all our services are insured to ensure that your goods are protected throughout the transportation process.

Do you provide international shipping?

Currently, we specialize in domestic transportation services. However, we are expanding our services to include international shipping in the near future.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support through our contact page, or by calling our dedicated helpline available 24/7.
